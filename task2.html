✅ Answer: To align elements horizontally and vertically in Flexbox, the following CSS properties are used: On the main axis (usually horizontal) justify-content: Used to align elements along the main axis. Values:

    center – center
    flex-start – left
    flex-end – right
    space-between – equal space between
    space-around – space around
On the cross axis (usually vertical) align-items: Controls the alignment of flex items along the cross axis in the flex container. Values:
    center – center
    flex-start – top
    flex-end – bottom
    align-self: Adjusts the align-items function for an individual element.
